CAME Connect is CAME’s cloud-based platform for managing and controlling automation systems remotely, such as gates, garage doors, barriers, video intercoms, and even broader home‑automation setups. It connects CAME devices to the internet through a small gateway (Wi‑Fi, LAN, or 4G), allowing both users and installers to interact with the system from anywhere.

For pairing: https://community.home-assistant.io/t/came-connect-new-custom-integration/927639/20
Values must be setup in the App -> Settings menu.
